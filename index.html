About Vishwastra AI

Built for Enterprise. Engineered for Trust.

Vishwastra AI is India's first unified, government-grade AI platform — combining AI reasoning, zero-trust security, 12-framework compliance, and workflow automation into a single integrated system. Founded by K Rama Chandra (MBA — IBS, ANU), VishwaX Technologies Pvt Ltd is building the AI infrastructure that Indian enterprises and government organisations need to operate at sovereign scale.

We replace 5–10 fragmented tools with a single intelligent platform — cutting TCO by ₹3.64 Cr annually per 1,000-user enterprise while delivering 99.99% uptime and military-grade security.
